Portfolio

Tonal

This site was built as a group final project for DevMountain to show off our groups front and back-end development skills. Users are able to change the song that is playing in the background by moving around parts of the environment. This required us to integrate Tone.Js as well as creatively use clip-paths in the environment. Users can also save the changes they have made to the environments and songs.


From the world view the user is able to see all the saved environments all users have saved and import them into their own environment to listen to and change.



Learn My Muse

Originally conceived as a business idea and fulfilled as a personal project for DevMountain, this site was my way of resolving the issue with finding music tutors. Integrating a large database to store the information of all users, teachers and students, Learn My Muse allows users to enter an instrument and location and returns teachers in their area. Students are able to review teachers so other students can filter by rating and read reviews.


I also integrated the MapBox API so users can see all the teachers in certain areas as well as get directions to teachers. Auth.0 was used to allow users to sign-up and login allowing them to review teachers and submit an application to be added as a teacher.



Silk Road International

Silk Road International is an international consulting company who needed a new site. Carrying over simple design elements from their older website, the new site displays all the same information in a clean and modern design.



Prosperity Education Route

A small static site for a muli-national education company built to spread information about the company in a clean and user-friendly way.
